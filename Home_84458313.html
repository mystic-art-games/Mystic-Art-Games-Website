​​The Sunken Secrets
of Atlantis Await!

​Enter the sunken world of Hexlantis - the last fragment of the legendary Atlantis, preserved beneath the ocean's surface in a gigantic, energy-pulsating dome. Explore the endless, procedurally generated depths constructed from millions of hexagonal tiles - build your own underwater cities, explore a world of extraordinary organisms, mine ancient orichalcum crystals, create incredible machines, and discover forgotten temples.

Your goal is to find humanity's greatest secret - the legendary Treasure of Atlantis. Some claim it holds secret knowledge that allowed the creation of a utopian world, others believe it contains Power Crystals capable of generating energy and powering an entire civilization. Supposedly, this treasure holds such immense power that it could return Atlantis to the surface… or sink the rest of the world forever.

​Climate change:
what’s the fuss all about?

​Rising temperatures, extreme weather, vanishing species - climate change is no longer a distant threat. It's here, and it's reshaping the world around us. Our environment, our health, our food systems, and our economies are all at risk. The decisions we make today will determine the future we leave behind.

Catastrophica is more than a game - it's a simulation of urgency, consequence, and resilience. You take the reins of a civilization standing at the edge of environmental collapse. Navigate complex trade-offs, balance short-term needs with long-term survival, and witness how your choices echo across generations.

​​Hexlantis Pillars

​Infinite Exploration

​Plunge into a vast, procedurally generated hex abyss teeming with real-world inspired biomes, uncovering ancient temples and bioluminescent wonders that spark endless curiosity.

​Creative Building

​Craft thriving solarpunk utopias under the pulsing dome using hexagonal prisms, orichalcum tech, and symbiotic machines—your imagination shapes the last bastion of Atlantis.

​Pure Joyful Fun

​Dive into addictive FPP/TPP sandbox thrills for all ages, where every hex discovery, build triumph, and narrow escape delivers heart-pounding excitement and laughter in the depths.
