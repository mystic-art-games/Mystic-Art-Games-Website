Climate change - what’s the fuss all about?

Rising temperatures, extreme weather, vanishing species - climate change is no longer a distant threat. It's here, and it's reshaping the world around us. Our environment, our health, our food systems, and our economies are all at risk. The decisions we make today will determine the future we leave behind.

Catastrophica is more than a game - it's a simulation of urgency, consequence, and resilience. You take the reins of a civilization standing at the edge of environmental collapse. Navigate complex trade-offs, balance short-term needs with long-term survival, and witness how your choices echo across generations.
